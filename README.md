## ðŸ“Š Customer Segmentation & Sales Analysis (Power BI)

This project focuses on analyzing customer purchasing behavior using demographic and transactional data.  
The dashboard provides insights into customer distribution by gender and age group, product category preferences, revenue contribution, and payment method trends.

Using Power BI, the data was visualized through interactive charts and KPIs to identify key customer segments and business patterns.  
The analysis helps businesses understand their customers better, optimize marketing strategies, and improve sales performance.

### Key Objectives:
- Analyze customer distribution by gender and age group.
- Identify top-performing product categories.
- Evaluate revenue contribution across different customer segments.
- Understand payment method preferences.
- Provide actionable insights for business decision-making.
